About Us

Tuticorin, or Thoothukudi, is a historic port city in Tamil Nadu, India, famous as the "Pearl City" due to its pearl diving history, serving as a key maritime trade hub connecting East & West. Located on the Gulf of Mannar, it's known for its natural port, salt production, spices, textiles, unique sweets like Macaroon, and its significant role in India's freedom struggle, especially with freedom fighter V.O. Chidambaranar.
